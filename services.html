Hearing Care Services

Complete audiology from diagnosis to rehabilitation — for all ages, all types of hearing loss

Hearing Assessment & Audiometry

Comprehensive audiometric evaluation in a calibrated sound booth to accurately measure the type, degree and configuration of hearing loss.

  • Pure-tone audiometry (250–8000 Hz)
  • Speech audiometry (SRT & SDS)
  • Impedance / tympanometry
  • Otoacoustic emissions (OAE)

Hearing Aid Fitting & Programming

Expert selection, real-ear verified fitting and digital programming of hearing aids precisely matched to your audiogram and lifestyle.

  • Custom shell / ear mould fitting
  • Real-ear measurement (REM)
  • Digital fine-tuning & programming
  • Trial period available

Paediatric Audiology

Child-friendly hearing tests for newborns, infants and children using BERA, play audiometry, OAE and behavioural testing.

  • Newborn OAE screening
  • BERA / ABR / ASSR testing
  • Play audiometry (18m–5 yrs)
  • Early intervention support

Cochlear Implant Support

Pre-operative candidacy evaluation, device mapping and auditory verbal therapy for cochlear implant recipients and families.

  • CI candidacy evaluation
  • Pre-surgical counselling & tests
  • Post-surgery device mapping
  • AVT & aural rehabilitation

Tinnitus Management

Evidence-based tinnitus management including Tinnitus Retraining Therapy, sound therapy, counselling and hearing aid-based masking.

  • TRT (Tinnitus Retraining Therapy)
  • Sound therapy & masking
  • Counselling & coping strategies
  • Hearing aid-based relief

Aural Rehabilitation

Personalised listening and speech-perception training to help hearing aid users maximise communication in everyday life.

  • Lip reading training
  • Communication strategies
  • Listening practice exercises
  • Family counselling sessions

Vertigo & Balance Disorder Services

Advanced vestibular diagnostics and treatment — most BPPV patients leave without dizziness after one session

BPPV Diagnosis & Treatment

The most common vertigo — displaced crystals in inner ear canals. Diagnosed and cured in 1–2 clinic visits with the Epley Manoeuvre.

  • Dix-Hallpike diagnostic test
  • Epley Manoeuvre (posterior canal)
  • Semont Manoeuvre
  • Barbecue Roll (horizontal canal)

Ménière's Disease Management

Long-term management of this inner ear disorder causing episodic vertigo, fluctuating hearing loss, tinnitus and ear fullness.

  • Dietary counselling (low sodium)
  • Medication coordination
  • Vestibular rehabilitation
  • Hearing aid support

Vestibular Neuritis & Labyrinthitis

Structured acute management and vestibular rehabilitation for inflammation of the inner ear balance nerves.

  • Acute phase management
  • VRT exercise programme
  • Gaze stabilisation training
  • Balance retraining

Vestibular Migraine

Specialist assessment for migraine-associated dizziness — a commonly misdiagnosed condition often confused with BPPV or anxiety.

  • Detailed diagnostic evaluation
  • VNG testing
  • Dietary & lifestyle guidance
  • Neurologist coordination

Vestibular Rehabilitation Therapy (VRT)

Structured exercise programme to retrain the brain and vestibular system after inner ear dysfunction — promotes real neurological recovery.

  • Gaze stabilisation exercises
  • Habituation exercises
  • Balance & gait training
  • Home exercise programme

Balance & Fall Prevention

Comprehensive balance evaluation especially for elderly patients, post-surgical patients and those at fall risk.

  • Romberg & Tandem Romberg
  • Fall risk assessment
  • Computerised posturography
  • Elderly balance programme

Speech & Language Therapy

Professional therapy for communication, language and swallowing disorders in children and adults

Auditory Verbal Therapy (AVT)

Teaching children with hearing loss to listen and speak using their hearing aids or cochlear implants — maximising spoken language potential from the earliest age.

  • Early intervention (6m–5 yrs)
  • Parent coaching sessions
  • CI & hearing aid optimisation
  • Progress monitoring

Language Delay & Disorders

Assessment and therapy for children with delayed speech development, language comprehension and expressive language difficulties.

  • Formal language assessments
  • Vocabulary & grammar therapy
  • Narrative skills training
  • School-readiness support

Stuttering / Fluency Therapy

Evidence-based fluency shaping and stuttering modification techniques for children and adults who stutter.

  • Lidcombe programme (children)
  • Fluency shaping techniques
  • Stuttering modification
  • Confidence building

Voice Disorders

Assessment and treatment for hoarseness, vocal fatigue, pitch disorders, voice strain and post-surgical voice rehabilitation.

  • Acoustic voice analysis
  • Vocal hygiene education
  • Voice therapy exercises
  • Post-surgical rehabilitation
