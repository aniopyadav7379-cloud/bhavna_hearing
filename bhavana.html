Hearing Care Services

Complete audiology from diagnosis to rehabilitation — for all ages

Hearing Assessment

Detailed audiometric evaluation using calibrated booths to precisely measure the type and degree of hearing loss.

  • Pure-tone audiometry
  • Speech audiometry
  • Impedance / tympanometry
  • OAE testing

Hearing Aid Fitting & Programming

Expert selection, real-ear verified fitting and digital programming of hearing aids matched to your audiogram.

  • Custom-moulded shells
  • Real-ear measurement (REM)
  • Digital fine-tuning
  • Trial period available

Paediatric Audiology

Child-friendly hearing tests for newborns, infants and children using BERA, play audiometry and OAE screening.

  • Newborn screening (OAE/ASSR)
  • BERA / ABR testing
  • Play audiometry (1–5 yrs)
  • Early intervention support

Cochlear Implant Support

Pre-operative candidacy evaluation and post-operative device mapping and auditory verbal therapy.

  • Candidacy evaluation
  • Pre-surgical counselling
  • Post-surgery mapping
  • AVT / aural rehabilitation

Tinnitus Management

Evidence-based tinnitus management using TRT, sound therapy, counselling, and hearing aid-based masking.

  • TRT (Tinnitus Retraining Therapy)
  • Sound therapy / masking
  • Counselling & coping strategies
  • Hearing aid-based relief

Aural Rehabilitation

Helping hearing aid users maximise communication through personalised listening and speech perception training.

  • Lip reading training
  • Communication strategies
  • Listening practice exercises
  • Family counselling sessions
🌀

Vertigo & Balance Disorders

Advanced vestibular diagnostics & treatment for dizziness, BPPV and balance problems

BPPV Treatment

The most common vertigo — loose crystals in inner ear canals. Cured in 1–2 visits with the Epley Manoeuvre.

  • Dix-Hallpike diagnosis
  • Epley Manoeuvre
  • Semont Manoeuvre
  • BBQ Roll (horizontal BPPV)

Ménière's Disease

Management of this inner ear disorder causing episodic vertigo, fluctuating hearing, tinnitus and ear fullness.

  • Dietary counselling (low sodium)
  • Medication coordination
  • Vestibular rehabilitation
  • Hearing aid support

Vestibular Neuritis & Labyrinthitis

Acute management and structured rehabilitation for inflammation of the inner ear balance nerves.

  • Acute phase management
  • VRT exercises
  • Gaze stabilisation
  • Balance retraining programme

Vestibular Migraine

Specialist assessment for dizziness linked to migraines — often misdiagnosed as BPPV or anxiety.

  • VNG evaluation
  • Case history & diagnosis
  • Dietary & lifestyle advice
  • Coordination with neurologist

Vestibular Rehabilitation (VRT)

Structured exercise therapy to retrain the brain and vestibular system after inner ear dysfunction.

  • Gaze stabilisation exercises
  • Habituation exercises
  • Balance & gait training
  • Home exercise programme

Balance & Fall Prevention

Comprehensive evaluation of postural control, especially for elderly patients at risk of falls.

  • Romberg & Tandem Romberg
  • Fall risk assessment
  • Posturography
  • Elderly balance programme

Speech & Language Therapy

Professional therapy for communication and swallowing disorders in children and adults

Auditory Verbal Therapy (AVT)

Teaching children with hearing loss to listen and speak using hearing aids or cochlear implants — maximising spoken language potential.

Language Delay & Disorders

Assessment and therapy for delayed speech, language comprehension and expressive language problems in children.

Stuttering / Fluency Therapy

Evidence-based fluency shaping and stuttering modification for children and adults who stutter.

Voice Disorders

Assessment and treatment for hoarseness, vocal fatigue, pitch disorders and post-surgical voice rehabilitation.
