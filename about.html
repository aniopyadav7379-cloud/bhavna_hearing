Our Story

Trusted Since 2010.

Bhavana Hearing & Vertigo Clinic was founded in 2010 by Dr. Amit Maurya with a single mission — to bring world-class hearing healthcare to patients in Hyderabad at fair, transparent prices. No upselling, no hidden costs, no pressure.

From a single room in Trimulgherry, Bhavana has grown into a multi-branch clinic serving patients across Telangana, Andhra Pradesh and Mumbai — with a team of expert audiologists and speech-language therapists.

Our Journey

14 Years of Growth & Service

2010

Founded in Trimulgherry

Dr. Amit Maurya opened the first Bhavana Hearing Clinic in Secunderabad with a mission of transparent, patient-first hearing care.

2013

Vertigo Services Added

Expanded into vestibular diagnostics and BPPV treatment — one of the first clinics in Hyderabad to offer comprehensive VNG testing.

2015

RTC X Road Branch Opened

Opened second branch at RTC X Road, making Bhavana accessible to central Hyderabad patients.

2018

Paediatric Audiology Programme

Launched dedicated paediatric hearing and speech therapy programme with BERA, OAE and AVT services for children.

2020

Kukatpally Branch & Home Services

Opened KPHB branch and formalised home visit service — bringing hearing care directly to patients' homes across Hyderabad.

2022

Mumbai Branch — Ghatkopar East

Expanded to Maharashtra, extending Bhavana's transparent hearing care to Mumbai patients.

2024

295+ Reviews · 4.8★ Rating

Reached 295 patient reviews on JustDial with a 4.8-star rating — a testament to 14 years of honest, quality care.
